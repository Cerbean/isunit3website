Review of Meowchi Plushies

About Me

Taro Meowchi

A plum colored cat plushie that has a small smile on its face. The color is beautiful but it is not a rare plushie. Even so, you can't go wrong with a classic color and simple features.

Zombie Meowchi

A green plushie that has black stitching over it along with exposed bone and blood. This is a limited edition Halloween plushie and even though it has some gore to it, it is as adorable as the others and its rarity is valuable.

Mini Meowchi

A mini, mint plushie that has a keychain attached to it. This is a small and transportable plushie that is perfect to hang on purses or backpacks. The cost is a bit high for the small product, but the convenience is worth it
